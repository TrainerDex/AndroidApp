TrainerDex provides leaderboards for your favourite monster hunting game!

Current features:
- Leaderboards
- Profiles

Features coming soon:
- OCR Recognition
- Deeper Discord integration

TrainerDex only relies on screenshots and does not log into your pocket monster hunter account. There is no risk to your account.
